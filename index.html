WHO ARE
WE?

At ACM-W, we are a community of passionate, curious, and driven women in technology, working to build a more inclusive and supportive ecosystem on campus. Our core team brings together a diverse set of skills—from coding and design to leadership and event management—united by the shared goal of empowering women to pursue, excel, and lead in tech fields.
